How Do We Make The Snuggle Scent of Fresh Beer?
Click on the ingredients to find out more.

Barley
We use organic roasted malt that improves foam, enhances viscosity, and contributes golden hues with a candy-like sweetness.

Yeast
Our yeast produces the banana and clove notes traditionally associated with German wheat beers and leaves the desired cloudy look of traditional German wheat beers.

Hops
Hallertau, as known as the "Noble hops", are typically mild in bitterness and offer beautiful, floral, spicy, honeysuckle aromas and flavors.
